MENTORPSHIPS

My approach to drawing

I teach classes based on THREE MAIN AXES that allow us to develop your personal style.

IN THE FIRST LEVEL we focus on the analysis of artists that you like: Who do you admire? What draws your attention to them? What are the aspects of their work that you find valuable? Which not? The use of color, line, light. we do an exhaustive analysis to take note of what that magnetizes you about other artists work. And if you don´t know what attracts you, weel, we can explore togheter and make and investigation about that!

THE SECOND AXIS is about analyzing your own work. From technical issues to creative aspects of your creative world. Here we begin to establish relationships between technique and expression, that is, that technique be at the service of expressing what you feel you want to capture in the drawing.

FROM THE TWO PREVIOUS LEVELS we can begin to define what would need to be improved so that your creative world be in tune with your drawings. We will talk about the psychology of color, about the emotions that the different strokes generate on the drawing, of the qualities of the landscapes or characters that you create, to learn to translate all your subjective world in the painting. In this way we can generate an objective analysis and thus establish precise objectives to achieve!
