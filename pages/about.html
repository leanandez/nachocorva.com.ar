I am an Argentine illustrator currently working for a video game design company based in Spain.
I trained as an illustrator through artistic workshops and did my career as an illustrator at the School of Visual Arts Martin Malharro.
My main interest is focused on the design of fantasy characters and settings (I am a fan of role-playing games and the fantastic worlds of Tolkien, D&D, Magic The Gathering, etc.).
I have developed my own personal method for creatively approaching such universes, and that allowed me to get into collaboration with various international artists through a scholarship that I obtained to improve myself, with the supervision of Peter Morbacher.
I believe that the creative act has many dimensions, and that the technique of drawing is only one of them, being as important as the generation of atmospheres, music, rhythms, expressions, history of each character, and many others.
I consider that the task of the illustrator is to soak up these elements in order to capture the heart of what he wants to express, and from there put the technique at the service of the imagination.
